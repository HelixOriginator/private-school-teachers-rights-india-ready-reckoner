About This Handbook

Many private school teachers in India face unclear contracts, arbitrary termination, unpaid benefits, and inconsistent service rules. This ready reckoner brings together the legal framework, board byelaws, and practical safeguards that schools are expected to follow.

Think of this as a practical field guide, not a law textbook. Quick answers. Clear rights. Actionable steps.

Who This Is For

  • Private school teachers (aided and unaided)
  • School administrators and HR staff
  • Union representatives and advisors
  • Anyone reviewing service conditions or termination issues

Important Note

Laws vary by state and affiliation. This handbook provides guidance and general information. For serious disputes or litigation, consult a qualified lawyer or labour/education authority.
